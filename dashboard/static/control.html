Strategy Improvement Filters

These filters are applied on top of the core strategy signals. They are already partially implemented — this panel controls which are active. Note: These settings are informational until wired into the live engine in Phase 6B.

VIX Regime Filter
Skip trades when VIX is in HIGH_VOL regime (NQ PF=0.49 in high vol). Validated in backtest.
Economic Calendar Skip
No new entries during FOMC, CPI, NFP, and other high-impact events.
GreenLight Score Gate
Only trade when market conditions score above threshold. Half-size at 50-70, full-size at 70+.
HTF Bias Gate
Weekly/monthly trend must align with trade direction. Blocks longs in bear weeks. Core edge.
Consecutive Loss Full Pause
After 3 consecutive losses, stop all new signals for the day. Note: AccountState already auto-reduces size (2 losses→0.5x, 3+→0.25x) regardless of this flag.
Half-Stop at 0.3R
Exit 50% of position at -0.3R adverse move. Reduces average loss from -1R to -0.65R. Priority 1.
